PART ONE - GENDER STORY

Men still dominate the data science world
We see more gender disparities in the STEM fields. This should not come as a surprise, but the data science world, like the rest of the tech world, has a heavy gender imbalance, as 79% identify as men, 19% identify as women, and 2% identify as others.

* There are 20,036 respondents to the survey.
* Others include "Prefer not to say", "Prefer to self-describe", "Nonbinary"
The gender gap in all earnings
In each range of salary, men are payed higher. But this is an overall comparison of payment disparity. Why is there such big difference among different gender types?
Will education, job title, and experience change the situation? In the following interactive graph, we will focus on breaking down salaries for different genders by various factors, such as the ones mentioned previously, in the quest of interpreting the gender gap.
